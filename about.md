<h1>About Android Apps Spot</h1>
<p>Android Apps Spot is an independent review hub focused on US Android users. We publish step-by-step install guides for apps that aren't always available through the Google Play Store, including side-loaded APKs and third-party app store alternatives.</p>

<h2>Editorial Standards</h2>
<ul>
<li>Every install guide is tested on at least one current Android device before publishing.</li>
<li>APK sources are checked against the publisher's official channels.</li>
<li>We disclose when an app is age-restricted, geo-blocked, or removed from Google Play.</li>
<li>No paid placements — recommendations reflect our own testing.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Coverage Area</h2>
<p>Our guides are written for users in the United States. App availability, age verification laws, and store policies vary by state — we note those differences where relevant.</p>
